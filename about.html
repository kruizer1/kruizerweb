Research and Development

Research and Development: Today, our R&D team consists of chemists and technicians focusing on the development of new products as well on improving existing ones. Our portfolio contains over two hundred products tackling most lubrication needs. We offer both, “off the shelf” and tailor-made products made according to customer specifications. Our R&D laboratories can help you solve almost any problem you may encounter concerning the lubrication of your machinery.

We aim to innovate constantly and to investigate new raw materials and production processes as well as developing new products to meet future trends

Lubricant Safety

Lubricants are chemicals and should be handled with care at all times. Respect strict personal and industrial hygiene rules. To avoid contact with the skin & body

Use oil-proof gloves
Wear clothes with efficient protection
Do not wear oil contaminated clothes
Use a protecting cream

Technical Support

We offer free of charge lubrication surveys at your site and from the information gathered can recommend suitable products for each application and a suitable preventative maintenance programme to ensure that all re-lubrication intervals and individual application parameters are met. We understand lubrication but we also aim to understand your industry and application needs in order to ensure you are using the best product and obtaining the ultimate performance from them.

Our personal and attentive service allows you peace of mind that we are available for you when you need us
